RPS Game

This is an app where you face off against an AI controlled opponenet in a game of Rock, Paper, Scissors. The player selects their choice of rock, paper or Scissors and the AI picks it's choice. The app records the winner and loser and keeps track with a scoreboard.

Movie Finder App

With this app, users can search for movies by typing in words or terms into the search bar then the app displays movie posters related to the term. The users can then click on the movie poster and further details of the movie will display in a modal. The app takes advantage of the OMBD api for the movies and information.

Technologies: Javascript, API, HTML, CSS

Gif Search App
